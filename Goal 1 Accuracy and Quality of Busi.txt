Empowering Business Owners with IBM ODM Training Sessions:

Schedule regular training sessions focused on IBM Operational Decision Manager (ODM) for business owners.
Customize the training content to suit the specific needs and understanding levels of the business owners.
Cover key concepts of ODM, including rule modeling, decision tables, rule execution, and integration with existing systems.
Provide hands-on exercises and real-world examples to illustrate the practical applications of ODM in their business processes.
Offer follow-up support and resources for ongoing learning and implementation.
